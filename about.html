Mission

At its core, skateboarding is about freedom of expression and creativity. With these key principles in mind, I wanted to create a web app that would help people beginning on their skateboard journey. The goal was to create a site that would guide people as they learned while still giving them the freedom to make their own decisions about what tricks they want to learn.
